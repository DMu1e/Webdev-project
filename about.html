About WorkBook

WorkBook was created by a team of ambitious university students with a passion for building tools that make academic collaboration clearer and more effective. We understand the challenges of juggling classwork, communication, and project updates across different platforms, so we built WorkBook to bring everything together in one simple, unified space.

From late-night coding sessions to pitch presentations, this project reflects our commitment to solving real-world student pain points—starting with student teams just like ours.
